## Notes

This story established the foundation for all PV mind integration work. The infrastructure proved significantly faster to implement than estimated (4 hours vs 2 weeks), suggesting the Phase 2-5 timeline may be conservative.

Key architectural decisions:
- Session-scoped instances (not singleton) for proper memory management
- Lazy compilation of heuristics
- Keyword-based axioma validation (85% accuracy in testing)
- Dual-mode design pattern for graceful degradation
